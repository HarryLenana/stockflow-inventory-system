StockFlow

Smart Inventory Management System

StockFlow is a modern inventory management solution designed to help businesses efficiently manage products, suppliers, purchases, sales and business reporting through a simple, secure and user-friendly interface.

About StockFlow

StockFlow is a modern inventory management system built using Flask, SQLite, HTML, CSS and JavaScript. The system simplifies inventory operations by providing tools for managing products, suppliers, purchases, sales, reporting and administrative settings within one integrated platform.

Our Mission

To provide businesses with a reliable, secure and easy-to-use inventory management solution that improves efficiency, minimizes stock losses and supports better business decisions.

Our Vision

To become one of the leading inventory management platforms by delivering innovative, scalable and intelligent business solutions for organizations of every size.

Key Features

Product Management

Manage inventory items with categories, stock levels and pricing.

Supplier Management

Store supplier information including contacts and addresses.

Purchase Tracking

Record purchases and automatically update inventory stock.

Sales Tracking

Monitor customer sales while reducing product stock automatically.

Revenue Analytics

Generate insights into business performance and revenue.

Data Export

Export reports in CSV, Excel and PDF formats.

Dashboard Reports

View business summaries through visual charts and statistics.

User Management

Role-based access control for administrators, managers and cashiers.
